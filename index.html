Emberfall, a playable arcade game where you defend a forest from falling embers and spreading fire. Tap embers and beetles to swat them. A toolkit on the left lets you select tools: the default Water tool douses burning trees, wets healthy trees, and knocks down dead snags; Seed plants free trees; Climate plants fire- and beetle-resistant trees from a stock that refills over time; Line digs a firebreak that blocks fire spread; Beetle sprays verbenone to repel bark beetles from an area; and Air calls a retardant tanker once you have enough points. Dead snags breed beetle outbreaks, woodpeckers arrive to eat beetles, surviving trees reseed burned ground with cones, periodic rain suppresses fire, and the fire intensifies as time passes.
